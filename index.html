About us

Located in one of the oldest coast part, Symphony is an authentic Italian restaurant specializing on Southern Italian cuisine and seafood. It is a very cozy place with a large diversity of dishes based on the best traditional recipes. Cobblestone streets, old fashion sailing ships which moored right to the pier, make you feel like you are in Sicily.

Ingredients

Fresh traditional ingredients, mixed with flavors of different cultures. Perfect materials and freshly baked food. This combination is the only key to success.
